Intelligent & Trainable - Very clever and obedient dogs

Care
Balanced diet & portion control – Feed twice daily with quality large‑breed food (Purina is a good brand) ; avoid overfeeding to prevent obesity and joint issues
Plenty of exercise – Aim for at least 1–2 hours daily (walks, fetch, swimming, agility) to keep the and prevent destructive behaviours
Routine grooming & hygiene – Brush 2–3× weekly (more during shedding seasons), clean ears regularly, trim nails, and brush teeth 2–3 times per week.
Early training & mental stimulation – Buys toys and other items they can play with and uses positive reinforcement to encourage obedience.
Veterinary care & health monitoring – Schedule regular vet check-ups to prevent health issues and consider pet insurance.
